Welcome to CT Aeronautics

Hello, welcome to CT Aeronautics. CT Aeronautics (originally ) was created in late 1915 as one of the first mass producers of aircraft. originally only built military aircraft for the Country of Corvania, but after the tensions died down, quickly struggled to find other countries that wanted their aircraft, soon was bought out by CT Enterprises and rebranded as "CT Aeronautics". CT Aeronautics was quickly transformed to take advantage of the quickly growing market for civilian air travel. As the years went on CT Aeronautics grew to make the most renowned aircraft of the 60s-90s, introducing civilian supersonic travel and jet airliners to the grand stage. Nowadays, CT Aeronautics is known to be the largest manufacturer of aircraft.
